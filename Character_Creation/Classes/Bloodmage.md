Bloodmages are cruel practitioners of the dark arts, utilizing the intrinsic magical potency of blood to perform forbidden rituals and cast twisted spells.

## Hit Die
Your hit die is a d8

## Starting Hitpoints
Your starting hitpoints are 8 + your constitution modifier

### Bloodpoints
You have the ability to transfer blood into vitality and vitality into blood. You are capable of expending a bonus action to take 1d8 true damage and gain a number of bloodpoints equal to the damage taken. You may also expend a bonus action to use up to 8 bloodpoints and regain that many hitpoints. The bloodpoints you gain dry up and disappear after 1 hour, each time you gain bloodpoints this timer is reset.

### Letting Blade
You are able to perform a ritual over a dagger during a rest to magically transform it into a letting blade. Whenever you deal damage to a creature with your letting blade, you receive bloodpoints equal to the damage dealt, as their blood is sucked into the blade's grooves and transfered to you. You cannot gain bloodpoints from a creature that is a construct or undead. 

### Hitpoint Increase
Your hitpoints increase by 1d8 + your constitution modifier.

### Bloodcasting
You gain the ability to perform special actions using bloodpoints at your disposal. You learn 4 blood-magic techniques, refer to the blood-magic techniques known column of the level chart to understand how many techniques you have at a given level. Whenever you take a rest you may perform a ritual to replace one blood-magic technique you know with another. Your bloodcasting ability is constitution or charisma (your choice) and your spell attack modifier is your proficiency bonus + your casting modifier for attack rolls and your bloodcasting DC is 8 + your proficiency bonus + your casting modifier.

### Hematologic Path (optional)
You reach a branching path in the progress of your blood magic. Choose one of the following paths to abide by or alternatively gain 2 Blood-magic techniques.

#### Mortification 
Only by the pain and death of your flesh will your power grow. You gain three times as many bloodpoints when damaging yourself for bloodpoints but half as many from using your letting blade on enemies.

#### Purification
Only by the purge of those who would stand in your way shall your studies advance. You gain twice as many bloodpoints from damaging enemies but half as many from damaging yourself.

#### Prudence
You will overcome only through strict planning and preparation. You gain the ability to store bloodpoints harvested in combat within vials. You may store up to 10 bloodpoints in a watertight container wherein they last indefinitely. You may retrieve bloodpoints from a container as an action. There is no limit to how many containers you may use to store bloodpoints in this way.

#### Extravagance 
Only through a grand show and great feats will your name be cemented in history. You gain twice as many bloodpoints while in combat from all sources but they only last until the end of your turn.  

### Minor Hitpoint Increase
At 4th level, and every level after, add your constitution modifier to your maximum hitpoints. If your constitution modifier increases by any means, reflect that change retroactively in your maximum hp.

### Ability Score Increase
You may increase one of your ability scores by 2 or two of them by 1. You may forgo your ability score increase and take a feat instead.

### Extra Attack
When you take the attack action, you may make two attacks instead of one.

### Blood Rituals
You gain the ability to use large amounts of blood to perform incredible magical rituals. By spending 10 minutes and expending 100 bloodpoints you form a sigil of blood on the ground casting one of the following spells. If the spell targets a creature then the target is any creature that sits in the center of the sigil at the end of the 10 minutes. 

- Invincibility, with the duration beginning once the creature is hit by an attack, but before being damaged.
- Shapeshift
- Regenerate
- Raise Dead
- Gateway, with the spell lasting until the sigil is broken.
- Conjure Demon, with the spell lasting until the sigil is broken or the demon is banished or dies.
- Summon Undead Horde

# Blood-Magic Techniques

### Transfusion
You give a sacrifice of blood to embolden the health of another. As an action you expend 3 bloodpoints to touch and heal a creature other than yourself for 1d8 + your casting modifier hitpoints.

### Bloody Thorn
as an action you may expend 3 bloodpoints to expel a thorn of condensed blood at a target within 120 feet. Make a spell attack against the target, on a hit the target takes 1d12 + your casting modifier piercing damage and you gain that many bloodpoints.

### Bramble Shield
As a reaction to being targeted by an attack you may expend 3 bloodpoints to gain a +2 bonus to your armor class until the end of your next turn when the shield crumbles. While the shield is active any damage that would have hit you otherwise but now misses due to your increased AC is reflected back on the attacker, who piercing damage equal to half the damage you would have taken as thorns expel from the shield.

### Blood Weapon
As a free action you may expend bloodpoints to miraculously form a martial or simple weapon from blood, as well as 30 rounds of ammunition for it provided it is a ranged weapon. The amount of bloodpoints expended is dependent on the caliber of the weapon you wish to conjure, refer to the following chart. This weapon breaks down and is destroyed after 10 minutes.

### Blood-sense
You gain the ability to sense heartbeats within 30 feet of you and you may expend 5 bloodpoints as an action to see all blood within 120 feet of you through any surface except silver.

### Bloody Tendril
You gain the ability to call forth rudimentary life from blood. By expending 5 bloodpoints you create a small puddle of blood from which a 20 foot long tendril sprouts. At the end of your turn you may control the tendril and have 30 feet of blindsight while directly controlling it.

### Bloodwings
You may expend 4 bloodpoints as a bonus action to conjure bloody wings from your back which grant you 40 feet of flying speed. The wings remain for 10 minutes and then crumble and disappear.

### Bloody Thorns
You may expend 8 bloodpoints as an action to conjure forth a 20 foot cone of bloody thorns that lash out from in front of you. Any creature caught in the area must make a dexterity saving throw against your bloodcasting DC or take 2d8 piercing and 2d8 necrotic damage, taking half as much on a success. You gain bloodpoints equal to half the piercing damage dealt to single target of your choice by this blood-magic technique.

### Hemokinesis
You may control blood as an action and use it to move objects no greater than 30 pounds in weight and to manipulate items from afar. The range of your ability to do so is normally 30 feet, but you may double this range at the cost of 2 bloodpoints per doubling. The maximum range is 1000 ft. There must be a free source of blood to use this ability, a wound or a dead creature would suffice.

### Thirsty Blade
Your letting blade yearns for bloodshed. When you have 3 or fewer bloodpoints on the start of your turn you gain advantage on attacks with your letting blade. 

### Blood Empowered Strike
You may use a bonus action to expend 5 bloodpoints and grant advantage to your next attack.

### Red Mist
You may use a bonus action to expend 3 bloodpoints and vanish in a red mist appearing in an unoccupied space within 60 feet.

### Stolen Flesh
As an action you may expend 3 bloodpoints to cast disguise self. 

### Letting sword
Rather than blessing a dagger over a rest, you may choose to bless a shortsword, allowing you to use it to siphon bloodpoints.

### Advanced Circulation
As a bonus action you may expend 6 bloodpoints and increase your circulation for 1d6 turns. While your circulation is increased you gain 15 feet of extra walking speed and ignore the effects of exhaustion.

### Natural Siphon
You perform a ritual to bless your natural weapons, damage dealt by any of your natural attacks siphons bloodpoints equal to the damage you deal with them.

### Crimson Armor
As a bonus action on your turn you may expend 8 bloodpoints to conjure a set of armor around yourself. The armor lasts for 1 hour and gives you an AC equal to 13 + your dexterity modifier. 

### Acidic Haze
As an action on your turn you may expend up to 20 bloodpoints and cast blood in a 15 foot cone originating from you. Each creature in the area of the cone must make a dexterity saving throw or take 3d6 + (2 times the amount of bloodpoints expended) acid damage, taking half as much on a success.

### Warmonger (level 5)
Rather than blessing a dagger over a rest, you may choose to bless a martial weapon, allowing you to use that weapon to siphon bloodpoints.

### Blood puppet (level 5)
As an action on your turn you may expend 10 bloodpoints to force another creature to make a strength saving throw against your bloodcasting DC or be made an unwilling accomplice as you take control over their body. Each round on their initiative count you control them, their turn ends with the creature making another strength saving throw ending the effect on themselves on a success.

### Bloody Slash (level 5)
As an action on your turn you may expend 10 bloodpoints to throw a blade of blood. all creatures in a 60 foot by 5 foot line originating from you must make a dexterity saving throw or take 4d10 slashing damage, taking half as much damage on a success.

### Bloody Mess (level 7)
As an action on your turn you may expend 15 bloodpoints to expand blood inside of one creature within 60 feet of you. The target creature must make a constitution saving throw against your bloodcasting DC or take 8d6+10 force damage, taking half as much on a success, on a failure you may target one limb of the creature, crippling that limb or outright exploding it if the creature is bloodied. If a creature is reduced to 0 hitpoints by this effect then they explode into a mess of blood and viscera.

### Bloodlust (level 7)
As a bonus action on your turn you may expend 10 bloodpoints and enter a frenzied bloodlust. For the duration you may not use any active blood-magic techniques but gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage and advantage on attacks with your letting blade. This bloodlust lasts 1 minute and you may end it as a bonus action.

### Piercing Blood (level 7)
As an action on your turn you may expend 20 bloodpoints to fire a concentrated beam of pressurized blood. all creatures in a 120 foot by 5 foot line originating from you must make a dexterity saving throw or take 5d10+20 piercing damage, taking half as much damage on a success.
